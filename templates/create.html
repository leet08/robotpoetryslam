  • Reopen the breakout rooms. Leads should share their screen, advance to the voting page, and type in voting responses. End all breakout rooms after the time limit is up.
  • Have leads click the Results link on their final page. Final scores will not calculate unless all teams click this link!
  • With everyone back in the main room, share your screen and go to the final results pages.
